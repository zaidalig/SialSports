Our Story

Founded by Saad Iftikhar, Sial Sports is built on a simple idea: world-class sports gear should be accessible, reliable, and crafted with care. We merge traditional techniques with innovation to support every athlete’s pursuit of greatness.

🏁 2023 – The Idea

Inspired by a gap in the market, we started designing gear that balances quality with affordability—hand-tested by local athletes.

🛠️ 2024 – Prototyping & Partnerships

We began sourcing materials and collaborating with factories to bring our first cricket and football prototypes to life.

🚀 2025 – Launching Sial Sports

Our first gear drop is ready. From English willow bats to stitched footballs, every item is built for performance and pride.
